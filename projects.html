2023

Built a predictive XGBoost model to forecast intracerebral hemorrhage (ICH) outcomes using time-series ICU data and PubMed radiology reports. Achieved 74.11% accuracy and 75.51% precision, aiding hospital decisions by identifying high-risk patients.
