Research

My research focuses on robustness and explainability of large multimodal models — understanding how microscopic input perturbations cascade through neural representations to cause failures in vision-language understanding, embodied navigation, and LLM reasoning.

NeuroShield-ViT: Robust Vision Transformers via Representation Vulnerability Mitigation
ISVC 2025 · Oral

International Symposium on Visual Computing (ISVC 2025)

Examined how small input changes affect layer-wise representations in Vision Transformers (ViTs). Proposed NeuroShield-ViT to neutralize vulnerable neurons and enhance adversarial robustness, improving classification accuracy by 54.85% against PGD attacks and 71.6% against IGO attacks on ImageNet1K.

Adversarial Exploitation in Robot Vision-Language Navigation
IROS 2024 · Oral

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS 2024)

Developed algorithms that exploit semantic representation gaps in vision-language navigation (VLN) models to redirect robotic agents along malicious paths. International Symposium on Visual Computing (ISVC 2025) · LNCS, Springer
